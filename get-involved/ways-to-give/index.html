By donating to CCF you WILL make a difference toward saving the cheetah in the wild. There are many ways to give. Make a donation, sponsor a cheetah, or support our research, education and conservation efforts with a bequest.

We have stabilized cheetah populations in Namibia, and with your help, we can take the success we've achieved to the rest of Africa.

Workplace Donation Matching

Many employers offer matching gift programs to match charitable contributions made by their employees—and sometimes gifts from spouses and retirees are eligible too!

Use the search tool below to see if your company will match your donation. You'll also find the forms, guidelines, and instructions you need to submit a matching gift. It's quick and easy—most searches take less than 30 seconds! Double your impact today and help secure a future for cheetahs and their habitats!
